# Distillation

A 3B model that runs on a phone won't beat a 405B that runs in a data center on benchmarks — but it's only ~5 points behind on most chat use cases, and it runs offline on $50 of silicon. **The art of getting there is distillation, careful pretraining recipes, and post-training.**
