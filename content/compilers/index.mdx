## Modules in this track

- **[Foundation](/compilers/foundation)** — LLVM IR, passes, MLIR, dialects, lowering. The substrate.
- **[Production](/compilers/production)** — torch.compile, JAX/Pallas, IREE/ExecuTorch, operator fusion. The compilers people actually run.
- **[Kernels & Hardware](/compilers/kernels)** — Triton, CUTLASS, ThunderKittens, the 2026 hardware landscape. Where you drop down when the compiler isn't enough.

## What you'll be able to do after

- Read MLIR dumps and understand what each dialect is doing
- Write a Triton kernel and a small MLIR pass
- Recognize when a workload calls for `torch.compile` vs hand-written kernels
- Tell which hardware (Blackwell · MI355X · TPU v6 · Cerebras · Groq) is right for which workload
