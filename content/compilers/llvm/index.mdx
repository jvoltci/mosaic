# LLVM

If you understand LLVM, you can read the bottom of every modern compiler: Clang, Rustc, Swift, MLIR, even ML compilers like TVM and XLA all eventually emit LLVM IR. The IR itself is small and learnable — and it's the universal language of "what the compiler thinks your program is doing".
